About Beic Motors

At Beic Motors, we believe that the thrill of the open road and the freedom of two wheels should be accessible to all. Since our inception, we've been driven by a passion for motorcycles and a commitment to delivering top-quality machines that redefine the riding experience.


Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to inspire and empower individuals to embark on incredible journeys with motorcycles that go beyond transportation. We strive to create motorcycles that not only meet the highest standards of performance and design but also embody the spirit of adventure and freedom that motorcycling represents.



Recently Launched R1

The R1 has been a true icon among sports motorcycles for decades. Born from a relentless pursuit of performance, the R1 represents the pinnacle of engineering expertise. Each generation of the R1 has pushed the boundaries of what is possible on two wheels, leaving a trail of championships, records, and awe-inspired riders in its wake.

The latest iteration of the R1 is a testament to the commitment to innovation and excellence. This superbike boasts a meticulously engineered 998cc inline-four engine that churns out mind-boggling horsepower and torque figures, propelling riders into a realm of exhilaration like never before. The finely-tuned engine is complemented by cutting-edge electronics, providing precise control and enhancing rider confidence.
